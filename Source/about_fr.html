Voulez-vous savoir un peu plus à propos de nous?

Le nom de notre cafe vient du nom de mon chien. C'est la raison pourquoi vous allez voir beaucoup des decoration avec des motifs avec des chiens dans notre espace clients.

Our cafe sells corean style desserts. Most of our customers enjoy the choice we have as well as the great quality of the coffee we offer.

We have a confectioner who makes all our deserts with lots of dedication. You can organize an event: a birthday party or a work team building. Our dessert artist will gladly lead you into the world of dessert decoration.

We prise the satisfaction of each customer and we care about your comfort during the time spent with us. We have a wide range of drinks that a custom made and taste like nowhere else.
