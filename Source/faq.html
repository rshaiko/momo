Frequently Asked Questions

Question.    When should i place order my cakes?

Answer.   We normaly ask for 2 weeks notice for a cake, cupcakes, cake pops, cookies orders.
We recommend not leaving it to the last minute!
-Wedding cakes:    Please place order your cake 1 months before the event!
It gives us enough time to make your perfect wedding cake.
-Custom designed cakes:   Please place order your cake 1 ~2 weeks in advance of your event.
We are sometimes able to accomodate last minute requests, don't be afraid to ask!

Question.   How much cost a cake?

Answer.   Prices will depend on the flavors, the design you choose and the amount of portions.

Question.   Do you deliver your cakes?
